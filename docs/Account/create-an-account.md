# Creating an account

## Desktop

To create an account on QuickJobs, you only need an email and follow these simple steps.

Creating an account is free and easy, just you need an email, access to [quickjobs.app](https://quickjobs.app) and click <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**log in**</span> located in the lower left corner.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/create-account/create_account_login_en.png" width="90%" />
</p>

A login form will open, click on <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Don't have account? Sign up**</span>.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/create-account/create_account_button_en.png" width="90%" />
</p>

A form will appear with the fields to be filled in, just fill them in and click on <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Create account**</span>.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/create-account/create_account_submit_en.png" width="90%" />
</p>

## Create account with google

Select the <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Access with Google**</span> option.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/create-account/create_account_google_en.png" width="90%" />
</p>

If you already have a Google account, enter your login credentials on the page that appears. If you do not already have a Google Account, click "Create Account" and follow the instructions to create a Google Account.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/create-account/google_form_en.png" width="90%" />
</p>

The first time you do this you will be prompted with the google confirmation modal. Just click <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Confirm**</span>.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/create-account/google_confirm_en.png" width="90%" />
</p>

After that you will be redirected to the quickjobs home page.
