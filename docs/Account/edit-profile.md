# Manage personal data

Are you ready for your dream job? In our application, you can highlight your skills and experience to find the job you desire. If you've recently acquired new skills or experience, update your profile and show what you can do. To increase your chances of being selected for a job opening, it's essential that your profile is complete and accurate. By doing so, you'll increase your visibility to employers and have more opportunities to land the job you've always wanted. So don't wait any longer and showcase to the world what you have to offer!

## Desktop
To manage your personal data, first make sure you are logged in, then access your profile page through the following route, Here, access to your profile.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/profile-setting-desktop-en.png" width="300" />
</p>

This is the profile page, Here you can edit all about your personal data, to this click on <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Edit profile**</span>.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/edit-profile-desktop-en.png" width="75%" />
</p>

There are different fields that you can fill in with new information. Just click on the "edit" button, add the relevant information in the different fields, and then click on <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**save**</span>. It's that easy! In no time, you'll have an updated and attractive profile for employers to see.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/edit-profile-2-desktop-en.png" width="90%" />
</p>

"Full name", "User name", and "Profession/Role" are the simplest fields to complete when setting up your profile. Just fill in the required information and click <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**save**</span> to update your profile, as shown in the example image.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/profile-edit-1-desktop-en.png" width="65%" />
</p>

In the <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**location**</span> option, there are two ways to input your location. The first way is to type in your location and use the autocomplete options to help you.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/profile-edit-location-desktop-en.png" width="65%" />
</p>

Alternatively, you can click <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Select location**</span> which will open up a map that allows you to select your location directly.
<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/profile-edit-location-2-desktop-en.png" width="65%" />
</p>

Once you've selected your location, simply click <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**save**</span> to update it.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/profile-edit-location-3-desktop-en.png" width="50%" />
</p>


The final stage before completing your profile! This is where you can add additional information about yourself and your skills. Show off your personality, career goals, and what makes you an exceptional candidate! In "Description," "Skills," and "Schema," you can include everything potential employers need to know to hire you.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/profile-edit-3-desktop-en.png" width="65%" />
</p>

Don't forget click <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Save**</span> when you finish editing the information.
