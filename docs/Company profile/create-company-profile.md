# Creating company profile

The creation of companies in Quickjobs to post vacancies is important for both employers and job seekers. Job seekers will have access to a wide variety of job opportunities, while employers can reach a broader audience of potential candidates to find the right person for the job. Join our application today and start exploring all the job opportunities we have available!

## Desktop
To create a company profile you have to access to your profile page through the followin route, first access to  <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**profile**</span>.
<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/manage-personal-data/profile-setting-desktop-en.png" width="300" />
</p>

On profile page click <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Create company profile**</span>.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/create-company-profile/manage-company-profile-desktop.png" width="65%" />
</p>

Get your company on the map! Fill out the fields below with your company's vital info, including name, username, website, description, and location. Don't forget to double-check your entries before submitting.

Complete the filds with your company information and click <span style={{color : '#f97316'}}>**Save**</span>.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/create-company-profile/create-company-profile-2.png" width="300" />
</p>
